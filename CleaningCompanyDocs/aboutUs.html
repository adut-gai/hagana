History

Hagana Cleaning Services Co. Ltd is a privately-owned business based in Juba, South Sudan. Established in 2022, the company began with humble beginnings, utilizing simple tools and equipment to provide essential cleaning services. Despite these modest initial resources, Hagana Cleaning Services quickly gained a reputation for reliability, thoroughness, and customer satisfaction. In just a short period, the company has seen substantial growth. From its inception, where the team used basic cleaning tools, Hagana Cleaning Services has expanded its inventory to include advanced cleaning equipment. The company now boasts a range of modern tools, including powerful vacuum cleaners and specialized carpet cleaning machines. This growth reflects the company's commitment to excellence and its dedication to meeting the evolving needs of its clients. Today, Hagana Cleaning Services Co. Ltd stands as a testament to the power of hard work, innovation, and unwavering customer focus, continually striving to provide top-notch cleaning solutions to the residents and businesses of Juba and beyond.
